Application Process

↓

STEP1. Apply Host family

You can apply for homestay through the above Application form. If you have any questions or uncertainties, please feel free to ask in the Q&A section. We look forward to receiving your application and welcoming you into our host family community!

STEP2. Receive email

After submitting your application, you will receive an email from Access Canada confirming the receipt of your application. Please carefully review the information provided in the email and respond accordingly. If you have any additional questions or require further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to us. We're here to help guide you through the process and ensure a smooth transition into our host family program.

STEP3. Home visit

We will visit the home and conduct a thorough inspection of the entire house, ensuring every aspect meets our standards. This includes examining the living room, dining room, private room, bathroom, and laundry facilities. Additionally, we'll assess the overall cleanliness, safety, and comfort of the residence to ensure it provides a welcoming environment for our students.
