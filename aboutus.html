ABOUT US

Access Canada Study Abroad Centre has offices in Toronto and Montreal, and is a company that arranges overseas study-related services such as homestays, ESL, public high schools, and airport transfers for Japanese students. Since its establishment in 2002, it has partnered with Japan's largest study abroad agents and has been accepting many students every year. Staff members are always available to support students, providing orientation upon arrival and 24-hours customer service, ensuring that students can live abroad with peace of mind. We are currently recruiting homestay families in the Toronto, Montreal, and Ottawa areas, so if you have a warm family willing to welcome Japanese students as part of their family, please feel free to contact us.
